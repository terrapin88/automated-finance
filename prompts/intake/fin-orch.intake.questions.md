- Org name?
- - Operating model: RIA / PE / HF / VC / FO?
  - - Timezone?
    - - Asset focus (crypto/public/private/venture/credit)?
      - - Approx AUM (optional)?
       
        - ## 2) Entity footprint
        - - Total # of entities (rough)?
          - - Counts by type:
            -   - US partnerships (funds/SPVs/GPs/management co)?

                    -   - Offshore feeders?
                        -   - SPVs?
                            - - Any master-feeder structures?
                              - - Any functional currencies besides USD?
                               
                                - ## 3) Service providers
                                - - Auditor?
                                  - - Tax firm?
                                    - - Fund admin?
                                      - - Prime broker(s)?
                                        - - Legal?
                                          - - Valuation firm (if any)?
                                           
                                            - ## 4) Custody + banking
                                            - - Banks?
                                              - - Custodians?
                                                - - Exchanges?
                                                  - - Wallet setup (cold storage providers, hot wallet policy)?

                                                      - - General ledger system?
                                                        - - Portfolio accounting platform/admin?
                                                          - - Reporting tools?
                                                            - - Expense/AP tools?
                                                              - - Data warehouse (if any)?
                                                                - - Document storage?
                                                                 
                                                                  - ## 6) Close + reporting cadence
                                                                  - - Close cadence (monthly/quarterly)?
